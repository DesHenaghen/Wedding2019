Dancing

The band will start playing at 8pm. Following our first dance, there will be a ceilidh until 9:30pm - traditional Scottish dancing. The word ceilidh comes from the Gaelic for 'gathering' or 'party' and party we shall! There will be demonstration dancers and it's very easy to pick up, I promise! There will then be a break until 10pm and then the DJ will provide disco music until midnight.
