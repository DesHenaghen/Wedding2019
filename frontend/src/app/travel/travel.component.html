Travel and Transportation

Below is some information to help some of you with the logistics of travelling to Scotland and where things are in relation to our venue. We will also create Facebook groups to help discussion and planning.

  • Jet2 has flights directly to/from Glasgow Airport on Mondays and Thursdays
  • Ryanair has flights directly to/from Glasgow Prestwick Airport on Saturdays
  • Ryanair also has flights directly to/from Edinburgh Airport on Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday and Sunday. There is a frequent bus link from Edinburgh airport to Glasgow city centre (£12.00 single, £20.00 return)
  • Although other routes may have layover, it's also worth checking Scyscanner if it's cheaper.
  • If you find a good alternative, please let us know and we can recommend it to other guests.
  • Easyjet has flights directly to/from Edinburgh Airport almost daily. There is a frequent bus link from Edinburgh airport to Glasgow city centre (£12.00 single, £20.00 return). The only problem we find is that they usually land late and the bus link is until 23:30, so if flights are delayed, it's hard to get to Glasgow in the middle of the night.
  • Ryanair does not have direct flights, but we found even with the connection, it could be cheaper!
  • Although other routes may have layover, it's also worth checking Scyscanner if it's cheaper.
  • If you find a good alternative, please let us know and we can recommend it to other guests.
