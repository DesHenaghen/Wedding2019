Accommodation

If you are looking for a place to stay on the night of the wedding, we would recommend either Western House Hotel - which is the wedding venue hotel itself or Premier Inn - it is conveniently a short taxi down the road.

The venue is in Ayr - a small seaside town. It is 45 min travel from Glasgow - Scotland's largest city. If you plan to stay longer, it might be best to stay more centrally other nights. We are here if you have questions :)
